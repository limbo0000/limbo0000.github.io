Biography

Ceyuan Yang is a first-year Ph.D student at Multimedia lab (MMLAB), Department of Information Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong. His supervisor is Prof. Bolei Zhou. Before that, He worked closely with Prof. Gong Cheng and received the B. Eng degree from Honors College in Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2018.
