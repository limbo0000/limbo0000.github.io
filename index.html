Biography

Ceyuan Yang is a second-year Ph.D student at Multimedia lab (MMLAB), Department of Information Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Bolei Zhou. He is very interested in representation learning, video understanding and generative models.

Before that, He received the B. Eng degree from Honors College in Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2018. From 2017 to 2018, He did an internship in Sensetime Group Limited with Dr. Zhe Wang and Dr. Jianping Shi. He also spent one year from 2018 to 2019 as a research assistant at MMLAB with Prof. Dahua Lin. Currently, He works very closely with Prof. Bo Dai and Dr. Zhirong Wu.
